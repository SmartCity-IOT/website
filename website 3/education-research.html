Learning Objectives

A Smart City is a modern urban area that uses technology, sensors, and data to improve the way people live, work, and move around. By connecting infrastructure like traffic systems, energy grids, and public services through the Internet of Things (IoT), smart cities aim to make everyday life safer, more efficient, and sustainable We are using these sensors as part of our Smart City cybersecurity project. Each sensor plays a crucial role in collecting real-time environmental data and helping us create practical datasets.
